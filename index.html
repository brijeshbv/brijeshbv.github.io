Our Mission

Vidhitsa in Sanskrit means, the desire to perform. This is what all parents and teachers aspire for their children and students.Five of us like minded friends have come together to form Vidhitsa Charitable Trust to aid hardworking children who want to study after high school but unfortunately find it hard financially to continue their dreams.


In our endeavour to give back to the society, we humbly promise you that we will do our best to aid as many children as we can who will hopefully have a better future by completing their
education.
